Golden Ratio in Flower Petals-- Number of petals in a flower is often one of the following numbers: 3, 5, 8, 13, 21, 34 or 55 which make this flower looks beautiful. Golden ratio in DNA Molecule.-- The DNA molecule measures 34 angstroms long by 21 angstroms wide for each full cycle of its double helix spiral. These numbers, 34 and 21, are numbers in the Fibonacci series, and their ratio 1.6190476 closely approximates Phi, 1.6180339.

Golden Ratio in Pinecones Spiral.-- Depending on the tree species, you can also see the Golden Ratio as Fibonacci number series in pinecones.
